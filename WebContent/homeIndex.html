RAM UTILIZATION

RAM is short for “random access memory”. RAM is one of the most fundamental elements of computing. RAM is the super-fast and temporary data storage space that a computer needs. The utilization of RAM by system is monitored and shown as graph to get the details of RAM consumption, Free RAM.

DISK UTILIZATION

DISK hold on to data even when the computer is turned off. A disk is a hard or floppy round, flat, and magnetic platter capable of having information read from and written to it. The most commonly found disks with a computer are the hard disks. The utilization of disk in the system is shown as graph.

CPU UTILIZATION

A central processing unit (CPU) is an important part of every computer. The CPU sends signals to control the other parts of the computer, almost like how a brain controls a body. The CPU is an electronic machine that works on a list of computer things to do, called instructions. The utilization CPU is shown as graph.
